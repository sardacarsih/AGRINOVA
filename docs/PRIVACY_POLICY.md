# Privacy Policy - Agrinova Mobile App

**Effective Date:** September 19, 2025
**Last Updated:** September 19, 2025

## Introduction

PT Agrinova Digital ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Agrinova Mobile application ("the App"). This policy applies to all users of our palm oil plantation management system.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **User Authentication**: Username, password (encrypted), employee ID, role assignments
- **Profile Information**: Full name, job title, department, contact information
- **Work Assignment Data**: Estate assignments, division assignments, reporting hierarchy

### 2. Device Information
- **Device Identifiers**: Device ID, hardware fingerprint for security authentication
- **Device Specifications**: Operating system, app version, device model
- **Network Information**: IP address, network connectivity status for synchronization

### 3. Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates**: Precise location data for:
  - Harvest block verification and mapping
  - Gate check location validation
  - Work area boundary enforcement
  - Estate and division location tracking
- **Background Location**: Limited background location access for:
  - Automatic estate check-in/check-out
  - Security boundary monitoring
  - Work hour validation

### 4. Camera and Media Data
**Camera permission is used exclusively for business operations:**

#### **Harvest Documentation**
- **TBS (Fresh Fruit Bunches) Photography**: Photos of harvested palm fruit for quality control and yield verification
- **Block Documentation**: Images of harvest blocks and work areas for record-keeping
- **Equipment Documentation**: Photos of harvest equipment and tools for maintenance tracking

#### **Gate Check and Security**
- **QR Code Scanning**: Camera access for scanning QR codes at estate gates and checkpoints
- **Vehicle Documentation**: Photos of vehicles entering/exiting estate premises for security logs
- **License Plate Recognition**: Images of vehicle license plates for access control
- **Driver Documentation**: Photos for visitor registration and security verification

#### **Quality Control**
- **Harvest Quality Assessment**: Images for quality grading and compliance verification
- **Equipment Condition**: Photos documenting equipment status and maintenance needs
- **Compliance Documentation**: Images required for certification and audit purposes

**Important Camera Privacy Protections:**
- ✅ Photos are taken only when you actively initiate the camera function
- ✅ All images are directly related to work activities and business operations
- ✅ Photos are securely stored on device with encryption
- ✅ Images are transmitted to servers only for business record-keeping
- ✅ No personal photos, facial recognition, or surveillance outside of work context
- ✅ Camera access can be revoked in device settings without affecting core app functionality
- ✅ Photos are automatically deleted from device after successful synchronization

### 5. Work Activity Data
- **Harvest Records**: TBS weight, harvest block data, worker productivity metrics
- **Gate Check Logs**: Entry/exit timestamps, vehicle information, visitor records
- **Approval Workflows**: Data approval/rejection decisions, workflow timestamps
- **Sync Activities**: Data synchronization logs, offline queue management

### 6. Communication Data
- **Push Notifications**: Notification preferences, delivery status, interaction logs
- **In-App Messages**: System notifications, work assignments, approval requests

- **Plantation Management**: Track harvest activities, monitor productivity, manage estate operations
- **Security and Access Control**: Verify user identity, manage estate access, maintain security logs

- **Harvest Verification**: Visual confirmation of TBS quality and quantity for accurate reporting

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage (Device)
- **SQLite Database**: Encrypted local storage for offline operations
- **Secure Storage**: JWT tokens stored in Android Keystore/iOS Keychain
- **Biometric Protection**: Optional fingerprint/Face ID authentication
- **Automatic Cleanup**: Temporary files and cached data removed after synchronization

### Server Storage
- **Encrypted Transmission**: All data transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption
- **Database Security**: Server databases protected with enterprise-grade security
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access restrictions and audit logging
- **Backup Systems**: Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures

### Camera Data Security
- **Local Encryption**: Photos encrypted on device storage
- **Secure Upload**: Images transmitted via encrypted channels only
- **Server Security**: Photos stored on secure servers with access controls
- **Retention Policies**: Images retained only as long as required for business operations

## Children's Privacy

The Agrinova Mobile app is designed for business use by employees and authorized personnel. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.
